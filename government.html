Government Services

We help employees learn, grow, and develop into effective leaders and managers within their department.

We seek to meet the needs of government agencies to develop their greatest asset, their people. Our services target training, management, education, and leadership needs. As a small, woman owned business, we understand organization and individual strengths and challenges within different agencies ... but understand that all have high expectations and outcomes.

Doing business with government agencies is an important part of our company. We bid on and provide services under government contracts on a regular basis. Of course, our conduct and services adhere to all security regulations and are implemented with professionalism and integrity.

We encourage your agency or representative to contact us for information about the science of non-verbal communication and how it impacts personnel, relationships, performance, and cultures within your agency.

Upon request, let’s discuss our company's past performance activities with federal agencies and current projects.

If you are another agency seeking potential partnership with a WOSB or EDWOSB, let us know! We value partnerships and look forward to growing America together one person at a time!

Evaluation & Research

  • Designing measurable (SMART) objectives
  • Refining your activities
  • Identifying and resolving challenges

  • Measuring outcomes
  • Celebrating gains and successes
